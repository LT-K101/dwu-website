Admission Requirements

Learn about the general admission requirements for undergraduate programs at Divine Word University.

General Requirements

  • Grade 12 Certificate or equivalent with a minimum GPA of 2.0
  • Minimum of 5 Grade 12 credits including English and Mathematics
  • Completed application form with all required documents
  • Non-refundable application fee
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Certified copies of academic certificates and transcripts

Additional Requirements

  • Letter of recommendation from a teacher or employer
  • Personal statement or letter of intent
  • Medical examination report (for certain programs)
  • Interview (for certain programs)
  • Portfolio (for creative arts programs)
  • English language proficiency test results (for international students)

For specific program requirements, please contact our admissions office or visit the program page.
